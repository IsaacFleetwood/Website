History of No Man's Sky

Image: The logo for No Man's Sky, featuring a red environment with a large diamond shaped floating object in the sky and a moon in the background.

I. Announcement

Back in December 2013, during the 2013 Spike VGX awards, Hello Games showcased No Man's Sky to the world, giving them the first preview into the universe of the game (Gaston 2013).

II. Release

Upon release, the game was met with a large amount of criticism, mainly for the amount of content that was present in trailers and discussed in press released that was not present in the released game.

III. Updates

Over time, Hello Games released new updates to No Man's Sky that added new features into the game to make it a more complete experience that fullfilled the promises set out earlier in the game's life.
