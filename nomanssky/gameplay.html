I. Languages

One of the most interesting features of No Man's Sky when it comes to giving the player information is that the alien races each speak their own unique language that, at the start of the game, the player doesn't understand. It is only through learning the languages by interacting with members of the race or digging through ancient ruins left behind by the race can the player start to understand words of the language and decipher what the characters they are talking with are saying.
